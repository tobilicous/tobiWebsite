About

Hello! My name is Oluwatobiloba (Tobi) Adesanya (she/her) and I'm a Nigerian American studying Computer Science and Business at the University of Southern California. I'm originally from Nigeria, but have lived in Texas and Florida. I graduated from West Broward High School. I enjoy listening to music, working out, and watching shows. I am most interested in working in Artificial intelligence, Software Development, Website Development, and Project Management. I am on the Partnerships team for HackSC, Southern California's flagship hackathon. I help to contact and negotiate the tiers for possible partners to ensure to raise money to run the hackathons, but I also occasionally contribute to the Engineering team.
